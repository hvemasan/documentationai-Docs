## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address or Google/LinkedIn account for sign-up
- Basic professional details ready (work experience, skills, education)
- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
</Callout>

ResumeUp.AI works entirely in your browser. No downloads required.

## Sign Up for Your Account

Getting started takes under 2 minutes. Choose your preferred sign-up method.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">
    1. Visit [https://resumeup.ai](https://resumeup.ai)
    2. Click **Sign Up** and enter your email
    3. Set a secure password
    4. Verify your email via the confirmation link
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google" icon="apple">
    Click **Continue with Google** on the sign-up page. Grant permissions for profile access.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="LinkedIn" icon="linkedin">
    Select **Continue with LinkedIn** to import your profile data automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
After sign-up, you'll land on the dashboard. Bookmark [https://app.resumeup.ai/dashboard](https://app.resumeup.ai/dashboard) for quick access.
</Callout>

## Create Your First AI Resume

Follow these steps to generate your resume.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Template" icon="layout">
    From the dashboard, click **New Resume**.

    Browse categories like **Modern**, **Creative**, or **Executive**. Pick one, e.g., Catalyst.

    <Image
      src="https://resumeup.ai/_next/image?url=%2Fimages%2Fnew_templates%2Fcatalyst_resume.jpg&w=640&q=75"
      alt="Catalyst resume template preview"
      width="640"
      height="800"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Input Your Details" icon="edit-3">
    Fill in sections:
    - Personal info (name, contact, LinkedIn)
    - Professional summary
    - Work experience (job title, company, dates, achievements)
    - Skills and education

    Use AI suggestions by clicking **Generate with AI** next to each field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate & Optimize" icon="zap">
    Click **Generate Resume**. Review AI enhancements.

    Run **ATS Check** to ensure `<95%` compatibility score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download" icon="download">
    Export as PDF or Word. Share via link or upload to job sites.
  </Step>
</Steps>
