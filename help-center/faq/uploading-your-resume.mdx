## Overview

ResumeUp.AI lets you upload your resume in PDF or DOCX format for analysis.

## Steps

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Upload Resume"
3. Select your file (PDF or DOCX)
4. Click "Submit"
5. Wait for processing (up to 30 seconds)

## File Limits

- Max size: 5MB 
- Supported formats: PDF, DOCX

<Callout kind="tip">
For best results, use clear formatting and common fonts.
</Callout>
