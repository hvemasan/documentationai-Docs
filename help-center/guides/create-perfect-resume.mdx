---
title: Generate an AI-Optimized Resume
description: Quick tutorial to create a resume using ResumeUp.AI.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start new project">
    Go to your ResumeUp.AI dashboard and click "New Resume".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your details">
    Fill in education, experience, and skills. Click "Save" after each section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a template">
    Browse templates and choose your preferred style.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let AI optimize">
    Click "Optimize with AI" and review the suggestions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download resume">
    Once satisfied, click "Download PDF". You're ready to apply!
  </Step>
</Steps>
